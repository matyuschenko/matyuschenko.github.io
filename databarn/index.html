About

DataBarn is a list of open data sources. We gather links to websites, describe them and power with search, which helps you to find the relevant data.

Our users are analysts, journalists, students, investigators, scientists and other people interested in working with data. Please send your ideas and suggestions via the form below or by email (datasitemailer at yandex dot ru).
